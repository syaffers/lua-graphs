Bubble Plot of Downloaded Rocks Count by Uploader

Nodes/Bubbles:

Nodes/bubbles represent an uploader and the size is relative to the amount of cumulative downloads of all rocks owned by that particular uploader. Hovering over the bubbles show the value of total downloads. The color of the bubbles are random and does not imply grouping.

Data from luarocks.org. Scroll your mouse over the graphs to zoom in.
