Bubble Plot of LuaRocks Count by License

Nodes/Bubbles:

Nodes/bubbles represent a license and the size is relative to the amount of rocks publish under that particular license uploader. Hovering over the bubbles show the value of total downloads. The color of the bubbles are random and does not imply grouping.

Data from luarocks.org generated using D3.js. Scroll your mouse over the graphs to zoom in.
