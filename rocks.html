Bubble Plot of All LuaRocks

Nodes/Bubbles:

Nodes/bubbles represent aa LuaRock and the size is relative to the amount of total downloads of the rock. Hovering over the bubbles show the value of downloads and the uploader made by the uploader. The color of the bubbles are grouped by uploader but since there are only so many colors (20 specifically), some colors represent 1 or more uploaders.

Data from luarocks.org generated using D3.js. Scroll your mouse over the graphs to zoom in.
