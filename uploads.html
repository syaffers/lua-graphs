Bubble Plot of Uploaded Rocks Count by Uploader

Nodes/Bubbles:

Nodes/bubbles represent an uploader and the size is relative to the amount of rocks uploaded by the uploader. Hovering over the bubbles show the value of uploads made by the uploader. The color of the bubbles are random and does not imply grouping.

Data from luarocks.org. Scroll your mouse over the graphs to zoom in.
